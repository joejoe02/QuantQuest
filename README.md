# QuantQuest: My Journey into Stock Market Machine Learning & Math
A hands‑on self‑designed course exploring the intersection of data science, mathematics, and finance.

In this repo you’ll find:
- Weekly mini‑assignments fetching and analyzing real stock market data
- Monthly projects building end‑to‑end models and backtests
- Math deep‑dives into returns, volatility, time series, and optimization
- Notes from lectures and curated resources
- A capstone project applying everything to a trading or equity analysis model

Follow along as I go from foundational concepts to advanced ML for stocks, blending theory with practical coding!
